COMMON QUESTIONS

ServerMiser is an all-in-one Discord application focusing on four core pillars: automated server setup (creating organized layout structures, categories, channels, and basic roles), active moderation protection (warnings, temporary timeouts, kicks, and bans), custom ticketing support desks, and gamified leveling systems complete with rank card renders and prestige roles.
ServerMiser is a title chosen for its sleek, high-contrast, brutalist aesthetic and memorable branding. While the literal definition of 'miser' relates to hoarding, our bot's true purpose is to act as your complete server administration and engagement partner — saving you time, de-cluttering manual management, and boosting user chatter.
Our automated setup engine is ServerMiser's main module. In 1-click, you can trigger |setup or |setup-custom to instantly establish organized categories, voice lobbies, ticketing support desks, staff channels, and basic roles. This eliminates manual configuration entirely.
